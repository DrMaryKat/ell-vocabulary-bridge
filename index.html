"What does the author mean when they say...?"
Vocabulary Scaffolds: author (writer), mean (trying to say)
Bridge Activities: Compare literal vs. non-literal meanings
Grade 1 Foundation Language
"The author is the person who wrote this story"
Building Blocks: Connect "author" to "writer" in home language
Visual Supports: Photo of author, connection to student writing
